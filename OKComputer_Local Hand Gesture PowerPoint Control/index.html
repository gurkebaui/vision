About the Technology

This hand gesture recognition system represents the convergence of computer vision, machine learning, and human-computer interaction. Built using state-of-the-art AI models, it enables natural, intuitive control of digital presentations without physical contact.

The system leverages MediaPipe's hand tracking capabilities combined with custom neural networks to achieve real-time gesture recognition with over 95% accuracy. All processing occurs locally in the browser, ensuring privacy and eliminating latency issues.

Perfect for presenters, educators, and professionals who want to engage their audience while maintaining natural movement and eliminating the need for traditional input devices.
